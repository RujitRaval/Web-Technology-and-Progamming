Shankersinh Vaghela Bapu Institute of Technology (SVBIT)

Shankersinh Vaghela Bapu Institute of Technology (SVBIT) was established in the year 2009 with the approval of AICTE and affiliated to Gujarat technological University (GTU), offering six courses in Bachelor of Engineering, with a total annual intake of 540. The college is situated at Gandhinagar – Mansa road, about 7 km from Gandhinagar, in a sprawling 40 acre lush green campus. The SVBIT is adorned with vast collection of books and journals. BGKV proudly launches second edition of “SPANDAN” a wall magazine, a unique identity of BGKV constituent colleges, which gives platform to technical students to discover the talent hide in them. SVBIT, with a view to provide opportunity to the aspirants, especially in Gujarat for their training in technical education and to provide consultancy and testing facilities to the industries / organizations located in this area and to join hands with Govt. of India in generation of technical man power. For the accomplishment of this noble task the college has been making consistent efforts with the help of innovative and committed faculty, technical and non-technical staff members and the students for the growth and progress. The college provides an environment which is not only pollution free but is also immensely suited for the holistic growth and the development of the students.

Shankersinh Vaghela Bapu Institute of Pharmacy (SVBIP)

Shankersinh Vaghela Bapu Institute of Pharmacy (SVBIP) was established in 2009 as self financed college approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and AICTE, New Delhi. The College building has 14 Laboratories including sophisticated instrument room and industrial laboratory, Four Classrooms, Tutorial Rooms, Auditorium, Seminar Hall, Library, Animal House, General Store & Administrative area, designed to suit as per the requirements of AICTE and PCI for pharmacy education.

OBJECTIVES AND LONG TERM GOALS :

To provide educational facilities for carrier in Pharmaceutical sciences for quality of education and research, improvement of human skill and health care system Accreditation of College To introduce post graduate diploma courses in the subjects of Pharmacy To Strengthen the industry-academic interaction To establish centre of excellence for pharmaceutical industries, educational institutions & research organization in and outside Gujarat state.

OUR STRENGTH :

The collage has appointed Principal & faculty with vast exprience, which is the key for development of respective department as well as provides motivation & inspiration to the students.
